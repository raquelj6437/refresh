Who Are We?

Our Mission

The Hand that Gives strives to provide shelters with specific needs that are not met through typical donation streams.


Our Story

Founded by Monica Shipman in fall of 2020, we are a group of Boston University students who wish to provide for the Boston community, specifically those who are experiencing homlessness and unstable housing.

Current Events

The Hand that Give is partnering with washloft (washloft.com) to collect and wash over 100 hoodies for residents at Boston Rescue Mission (www.brm.org), an organization that provides emergency overnight shelter to those in need in the Greater Boston Area.

For Boston Students on campus or those near Boston University:

Meet us from 5-6pm at Marsh Plaza on Dec 4, 9, and 11!
